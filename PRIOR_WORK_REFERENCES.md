## Notes

- Items marked **(index/log duplicate)** echo a claim from a concept page; fixing the concept page may require updating the index/log too.
- Suggested replacement patterns:
  - "first …" → "earliest known …"
  - "original …" → "earliest known …"
  - "pioneered by X" → "early work by X"
  - "introduced by X" → "described by X" or "presented by X"
  - "coined by X" → "the term appears as early as X"
